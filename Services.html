Styles and Services






Wigs Installs

Closures and frontals are hair pieces that blend in with your skin, giving the ~illusion~ that your natural hair is growing from your scalp and not a wig or a weave. You can switch up your look while also keeping all of your natural hair tucked away from any heat or style manipulation

Shampoo & Blow-Out

A blowout service includes a hair wash and then a blow dry with advanced blowout techniques and iron work to make your hair soft, shiny and voluminous!

Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning is a procedure in which your hair is coated and treated with nourishing products. It restores your hair's moisture, strengthens it and reduces the damage caused to it by chemicals and styling products.

Book Now


Twists

Two-strand twists are an easy, beautiful protective style. They can help lock in moisture. flat twists work well on different textures, two-strand twists generally work better on completely natural hair as the ends usually hold together without additional help from bands or barrettes. Twists can also be combined with other styles, such as braids, and afros.

Book Now
Sew In

Sew-in weaves are a process where your natural hair braided down into cornrows and a needle and thread are then used to sew down hair extensions onto the braids. With a sew-in, your natural hair is tucked away, so it requires very little upkeep.

Book Now
Double Process

A double process color refers to a two-step service. This is generally done by either lightening the hair first, then going in after and applying a color or glaze, OR this is done by applying a color then following up again with a color (permanent, demi, or semi).
